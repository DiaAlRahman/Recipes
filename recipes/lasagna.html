Lasagna

Description

This recipe will have your mouth watering, believe me! In just about 4 hours mostly unattended time, you will have your cheesy, meaty lasagna ready to eat!

Ingredients

  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Onions
  • Celery

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Make Meat Sauce
  3. Make Cheese Sauce
  4. Add everything together
